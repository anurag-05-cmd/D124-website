Our Mission

To provide a supportive and positive learning experience in which members are empowered to develop communication and leadership skills, resulting in greater self-confidence and personal growth.

Our Vision

To be the first-choice provider of dynamic, high-value, experiential communication and leadership skills development.

Our Values

Integrity, Respect, Service, and Excellence guide everything we do as we support our members' journey to becoming confident communicators and leaders.

Regional Coverage

District 124 proudly serves members across three beautiful countries, each with rich cultures and traditions. Our clubs span from the bustling cities of Eastern India to the serene landscapes of Bhutan and the vibrant communities of Bangladesh.

Eastern India

Serving major cities and towns across Eastern India with diverse club options.

Bangladesh

Supporting the growing Toastmasters community throughout Bangladesh.

Bhutan

Fostering leadership development in the beautiful kingdom of Bhutan.
